Psyche

Discovery Details

Discovery Date: March 17, 1852

Physical State

Diameter: ~226 km
Composition: Rich in metal (iron and nickel), believed to be the exposed core of a protoplanet.
Surface: Likely to be metallic, though this will be confirmed by the upcoming NASA Psyche Mission.

Temperature

Temperature Range: Between -73°C and -143°C

Orbital Speed

Speed: ~17.9 km/s

Interesting Fact

NASA is planning a mission to explore Psyche.
This mission may help us understand planetary cores.
