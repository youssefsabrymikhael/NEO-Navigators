Eros

Discovery Details

Discovery Date: August 13, 1898

Physical State

Length: ~34 km
Composition: Silicate rock, with metals such as iron and magnesium
Surface: Cratered, elongated shape.

Temperature

Surface Temperature: Ranges from -150°C to 100°C

Orbital Speed

Speed: ~24.36 km/s

Interesting Fact

NEAR Shoemaker mission landed on Eros in 2001.
This made it the first asteroid landing.
