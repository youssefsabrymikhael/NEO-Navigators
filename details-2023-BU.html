2023 BU

Synopsis

A near-Earth asteroid of this type was found on January 24, 2023, in Arizona by the Mt. Lemmon Survey.

Physical attributes

Size: Estimated diameter ranges from 3.5 to 8.5 meters (11 to 28 ft).
Composition: Appears to be a stony asteroid based on available information.
Velocity: At its closest approach to Earth, its velocity was approximately 9.6 km/s, or 21,500 mph.

The Closest Approach

Date: 27 January 2023
Distance: Reached one of the closest passes ever recorded by a NEO, passing approximately 3,600 km (2,200 miles) from Earth's surface.

Possible Hazards

Details on potential hazards will be assessed by space organizations to ensure monitoring of near-Earth objects like 2023 BU.
