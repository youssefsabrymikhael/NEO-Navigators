Asteroid Psyche

Overview

Asteroid Psyche is a unique near-Earth object believed to be primarily composed of metallic iron and nickel, offering insights into the building blocks of planetary formation.

Key Details

Diameter: Approximately 226 kilometers (140 miles).
Composition: Primarily metallic iron and nickel, similar to Earth's core.
Classification: Classified as a metallic asteroid located in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter.

Orbital Dynamics

Orbital Period: Completes an orbit around the Sun approximately every 5.5 years.
Path: Orbits within the main asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter.

Scientific Exploration

NASA plans to send a mission to explore Psyche, aiming to understand its composition, surface properties, and its history as a remnant of a protoplanet.
