Asteroid 2021 QM1

Overview

Asteroid 2021 QM1 is a near-Earth object that was discovered on August 24, 2021. It has garnered significant interest due to its close approaches to Earth.

Key Details

Diameter: Estimated to be between 50 to 100 meters (164 to 328 feet).
Composition: Likely composed of rock and metals, similar to most asteroids.
Classification: Classified as a Near-Earth Object (NEO) and is part of the Apollo group of asteroids.

Orbital Dynamics

Orbital Period: Completes an orbit around the Sun approximately every 1.3 years (about 485 days).
Path: Crosses the orbit of Earth and other asteroid belts.
Approach Velocity: Expected speed during close approaches is about 12.7 km/s (around 28,400 mph).

Potential Threat

Initially considered a potential impact threat due to its close approach in 2052. Subsequent observations confirmed that there is no risk of collision.

Scientific Exploration

Asteroid 2021 QM1 is a subject of study for scientists to understand asteroid characteristics and assess potential risks they may pose. It is regularly monitored by programs like NASA's Near-Earth Object (NEO) program.
