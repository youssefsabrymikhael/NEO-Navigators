Ceres

Discovery Details

Discovery Date: January 1, 1801

Physical State

Diameter: ~940 km (largest asteroid in the asteroid belt)
Composition: Rock and ice
Surface: Mostly composed of water ice beneath a dusty, rocky crust

Temperature

Ranges: ~-38°C to -105°C

Orbital Speed

Speed: ~17.9 km/s

Interesting Fact

Ceres is also classified as a dwarf planet.
It is the largest object in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter.
