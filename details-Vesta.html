Vesta

Discovery Details

Discovery Date: March 29, 1807

Physical State

Diameter: ~525 km
Composition: Rocky, differentiated body with a crust, mantle, and core (similar to Earth’s structure)
Surface: Heavily cratered, includes a giant impact crater called Rheasilvia.

Temperature

Ranges: Between -20°C and -190°C

Orbital Speed

Speed: ~19.34 km/s

Interesting Fact

Vesta is the second-largest object in the asteroid belt.
It was studied by NASA's Dawn spacecraft.
