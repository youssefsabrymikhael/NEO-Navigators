Bennu

Discovery Details

Discovery Date: September 11, 1999

Physical State

Diameter: ~490 meters
Composition: Carbon-rich with a rubble pile structure
Surface: Covered with boulders and loose material.

Temperature

Surface Temperature: Ranges from -40°C to 100°C (depending on sunlight exposure)

Orbital Speed

Speed: ~28.9 km/s

Interesting Fact

Bennu was the target of NASA's OSIRIS-REx mission.
This mission collected a sample from its surface.
